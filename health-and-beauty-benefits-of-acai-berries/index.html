22 Amazing Health And Beauty Benefits of Acai Berries

  • by Bharat Sharma
  • May 20, 2021

Acai berries are delicious fruit berries that provide several health and beauty benefits due to the presence of essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, and polyphenols, etc in them. The first known use of these round reddish-purple berries is by natives of amazon tribes for curing several ailments. These berries are mainly found in the rain forests of South America 

What Are The Nutritional Values Of Acai Berries

Eating 100 grams of pure acai berries provides

  • 70 calories of energy
  • 5 grams of fat
  • 1 gram of protein
  • 1 gram of dietary fiber
  • 4 grams of carbohydrates
  • 750 IU of vitamin A
  • 20 milligrams of calcium

Because of the essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, etc in the acai berries, they provide several health and beauty benefits like improved cardiovascular system, better immunity, reduced cholesterol risk, etc.

Health Benefits Of Acai Berries

(1) Helps In Removing Toxins From Our System

The detoxifying properties of acai berries aids in the elimination of toxins, pollutants, impurities, and other unwanted substances from our bodies. 

Vitamin C and other antioxidantal compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenols in the acai berries protect our cell and tissues from the free radical damage, and also helps with the efficient elimination of toxins from our system.

Dietary fibers in the acai berries also help with detoxification.

By helping with the detoxification, acai berries reduce the level of toxins and other unwanted substances from our bodies. When there are fewer toxins in our system, it reduces the burden of detoxification from our vital organs like the liver and kidneys to some extent and promotes other functions they need to perform. 

(2) Aids In Weight Loss

Consuming acai berries in moderation helps with weight loss because of the various nutritional compounds in them.

The fatty acid in acai berries speeds up metabolism and helps in getting rid of stubborn fat(excessive calories).

The dietary fibers in the acai berries provide satiety which means it keeps our stomach fuller for a longer duration, decreases our urge to eat again and again, and thus helps in controlling overeating which is a prime cause of obesity.

Eating 100 grams of acai berries provides 70 calories of energy which is neither too high nor too low.

(3) Regulates Blood Sugar Level

Moderate consumption of acai berries is good for diabetic patients and also for individuals who are at the risk of developing diabetes.

Acai berries have a low glycemic index which means they slowly release sugar into the bloodstream, prevent a sudden spike in blood sugar levels, and thus aids in managing diabetes.

Dietary fibers in the acai berries slow down the absorption of sugar by the bloodstream, and thus regulates blood sugar level.

Acai Berry Vs. Goji Berry – Which Is Better?

Both acai berries and goji berries provide several health and beauty benefits to us because of their rich nutritious profile. Both these berries are loaded with essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, polyphenols, and dietary fiber, etc. 

However, they also differ from each other in many ways. 

Some of the major differences between acai berries and goji berries are as follows:-

  1. In comparison to goji berries, acai berries have a higher number of antioxidants. However, when it comes to fat content, goji berries win over acai berries.
  2. Goji berries have a slightly higher number of nutrients like vitamin A, vitamin C, iron and calcium, etc in comparison to acai berries.
  3. Acai berries have less sugar and carbohydrates as compared to goji berries, and are thus beneficial for people with diabetes.

These berries complement each other and it is best to consume them together.

How To Select And Store Acai Berries

As we learned from the article, acai berries provide several health and beauty benefits because of their rich nutritious profile. They can be consumed as fruit and juice, and also in the form of tablets and capsules. 

Before consuming acai berries for their amazing benefits, it is important to ensure that you are not allergic to them. Also, consume them in moderation to avoid the risk of possible side effects. 

Acai berries supplements mostly have a shelf life of one year but it may vary according to brand and the product. Acai berries supplements should be kept in a cool and dry place that’s away from direct sunlight. 

In their natural form, acai berries should be kept in the refrigerator and should be consumed within one week. 

However, acai berry juice should be consumed immediately after juicing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Do Acai Berries Taste Like?

Acai berries are tropical and bitter and leave an after-task similar to that of dark chocolate.

How Much Acai Berry Juice Can One Drink?

The safe proportion to consume acai berries is juice is about quarter-cup of juice per day. It is suggested to consume acai berries as soon as you juice the berries. Higher consumption of acai berry juice is not good because of the higher concentration of sugar.

How To Use Acai Berry Powder?

Acai berry powder is obtained by crushing the dried acai berries. They can be easily blended into juices or smoothies, and as well as sprinkled over oatmeal or yogurt. Acai berry powder is best suited for people who find it difficult to get fresh acai berries in their region.

If you would like to consume acai berry powder in smoothies or juice, it is suggested to limit the quantity of added sugar.

What Are The Health Benefits Of Acai Berry Tea?

Acai berry tea is as beneficial as acai berry fruit or juice. It provides amazing benefits such as boosts energy, improves stamina, helps with weight loss, fights cancer, provides anti-aging benefits, helps with detoxification, and strengthens the immune system.
