23 Amazing Health And Beauty Benefits Of Clementines

  • by Bharat Sharma
  • November 15, 2021

Clementine is a hybrid citrus fruit between the willow leaf mandarin orange and sweet orange. It is deep orange and has a smooth and glossy appearance. They can easily be peeled into 7-14 segments. it is less acidic than orange and has a sweet and juicy flavor.

They were initially grown at the citrus research center, before their introduction to California commercial agriculture in 1914.

Clementines are of two types:-

  1. Seedless clementines- They have no seeds or fewer seeds. They can’t pollinate on their own and needs to be cross-pollinated.
  2. Seeded clementines- They have seeds and can self-pollinate. On average, they are bigger and sweeter than seedless clementines.

Clementines provide many health and beauty benefits as they contain many nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, and dietary fibers, etc.

Clementines contain potassium, a vital mineral that helps in maintaining our electrolyte balance.
