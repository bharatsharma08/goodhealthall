38 Amazing Health & Beauty Benefits Of Quail Eggs

  • by Bharat Sharma
  • May 17, 2021

Quail eggs are the mid-sized eggs laid by the various species of quail and are mainly found in Europe, North Africa, the Southern United States, and some parts of Asia. These eggs are smaller than duck eggs or chicken eggs. Due to the abundance of essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, etc. in the quail eggs, they provide several health and beauty benefits.

Health Benefits Of Quail Eggs

Some of the significant health benefits of quail eggs are as follows.

(1) Good For Eyes

Antioxidants like vitamin A and other antioxidantal compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenol in the quail eggs protect our eyes from the oxidative damage caused by the free radicals, and thus reduces the risk of age-related macular degeneration, cataract, and glaucoma, etc. 

Vitamin A is an essential mineral for our eyes as it keeps our eyes healthy and vision sharp. Deficiency of this vital mineral may increase the risk of night blindness and other eye disorders.

(2) Manages Cholesterol Level

Eating quail eggs regularly (in moderation) aids in managing cholesterol by reducing the level of bad LDL cholesterol and increasing the level of good HDL cholesterol.
Around 60 percent of the total fat in quail eggs is good cholesterol.
They prevent our arteries from getting clogged up by plaque and fat deposits, and thus reduces the risk of atherosclerosis, a leading cause of various cardiovascular problems like heart attack, heart stroke, and coronary artery disease. 

(3) Good For Growth

Quail eggs are rich in protein, a vital compound that helps in the growth and development of the body.

It plays an essential role in the growth and the repair of the cells, tissues, bones, muscles and blood vessels, etc.

(4) Provides Relief From Hypertension

“Potassium”, a vital mineral present in the quail egg is a natural vasodilator that relaxes our blood vessels, improves blood circulation, and provides relief from hypertension or high blood pressure.

Hypertension or high blood pressure is a silent killer that affects millions of people around the world and is a leading cause of various cardiovascular problems like heart attack, heart stroke, and coronary artery disease. 

(5) Helps In Detoxification

Another reason to consume quail eggs is because of their detoxifying properties.

The antioxidants and other antioxidantal compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenols in the quail eggs protect our cells and tissues from the free radical damage ( a kind of toxin) and thus aids with the detoxification.

The amino acids and water (around 70 percent) in the quail eggs also helps in getting rid of toxins and other unwanted substances from our body. 

Efficient elimination of toxins from our bodies improves our overall health.

(6) Provides Antioxidantal Benefits

Vitamin A and other antioxidantal compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenols in the quail eggs protect our cells and tissues from the oxidative damage caused by the free radicals, and thus reduces the risk of various cancers like colon cancer, abdominal cancer, intestinal cancer, and pancreatic cancer. 

Free radicals are the unstabilized ions that stabilize by stealing electrons from the neighboring molecules, and while doing so, they cause oxidative damage to them.

Antioxidants in the quail eggs also provide several other benefits like strengthening the immune system, improving cardiovascular health, reducing the risk of neurodegenerative diseases, etc.

(7) Soothe Allergies

The ovomucoid protein present in the quail eggs is an anti-allergy compound that soothes allergies and provides quick relief from various symptoms of allergic reactions like congestion and inflammation etc.

(8) Boosts Metabolism

The B vitamins present in the quail eggs improve metabolism and provides several benefits.

A healthy metabolism helps with the proper digestion of food, and also helps to burn calories at an efficient rate. This contributes to proper weight management. 

A healthy metabolism also ensures that we get proper nourishment from the food that we eat.

Vitamin B complex also provides several other benefits like improved muscle tone, improved cognitive functions, and proper cell growth, etc. 

(9) Provides Energy Boost

Essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, phytonutrients, and polyphenols, etc in the quail eggs make it a perfect breakfast food. 

It provides us the required “Energy Boost” for our day-to-day activity and is a healthier alternative to caffeinated energy drinks or beverages like tea or coffee.

(10) Good For Brain

(14) Keeps Our Cardiovascular System Healthy
