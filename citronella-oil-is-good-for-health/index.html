Citronella oil is a popular essential oil made from a type of lemon grass called Cymbopogon Nardus. The oil is made by steam distilling the tall, thin leaves of Cymbopogon. It smells sweet and refreshing and is thin.

 It is one of the most popular oils used in aromatherapy, and it has many benefits for your health, skin, and hair.
