  • by Bharat Sharma

Cranberries are the fruit berries that belongs to the evergreen dwarf shrub. It belongs to subgenus Oxycoccus of the genus Vaccinium. It is a major commercial crop and is cultivated in various American states and Canadian provinces. Cranberries are mostly processed and used as juice, sauce, and jam.

Being a good source of many nutrients like vitamins, minerals, antioxidants, dietary fibers and other compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenols, etc. they provide a number of benefits. This benefit of cranberries is mainly because of a large amount of water present in the cranberries. Cranberries are beneficial not only for scalp hydration but for maintaining the overall hydration of our body.

Not only eating cranberries, but topical application of cranberries is also effective in maintaining scalp hydration. For this, mash some cranberries and apply the mashed paste on your scalp. Massage it into your scalp for a few minutes. Let it air dry for 30 minutes, and then wash it off with water.
