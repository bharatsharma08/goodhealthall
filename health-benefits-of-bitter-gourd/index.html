Health Benefits Of Bitter Gourd

Some of the major health benefits of bitter gourd are as follows

(1) Keeps Our Digestive System Healthy

Bitter gourd is good for our stomach and improves our digestive health.

The laxative properties of the dietary fibers in bitter gourd improves our bowel movement, helps proper elimination of stool, and thus provides relief from constipation and other gastrointestinal problems like abdominal pain, irritable bowel syndrome, gas, bloating, and flatulence, etc.

Vitamin C, vitamin A and other antioxidantal compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenols protect our digestive system from free radical damage and minimizes the risk of colon cancer, abdominal cancer, intestinal cancer, stomach cancer, and pancreatic cancer, etc.

The anti-inflammatory properties of vitamin C reduce stomach inflammation and minimize the risk of ulcers.

The anthelmintic compounds in bitter gourd kill infections worms in our stomach and keep digestive problems at bay.

The antimicrobial properties of the bitter gourd protect the digestive system from microbial infections and diseases.

(2) Boost Our Immune System

Bitter gourd strengthens our immune system, the defense mechanism of the body that prevents and treats microbial infections, injuries, diseases, and wounds. 

Antioxidants like vitamin A, vitamin C, and other relevant antioxidantal compounds like phytonutrients and polyphenols protect our immune cells aka white blood cells from free radical damage, and thus strengthens our immunity.

Vitamin C enhances the production of white blood cells in the body and further strengthens our immunity. 

With stronger immunity, our risk of getting a cough, cold, fever, and flu, etc reduces drastically.

The antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal properties of the bitter gourd protect us from the ill effects of microbial infections and further strengthens immunity.

Other nutrients like iron, zinc, potassium, magnesium, and B vitamins in bitter gourd also contribute to stronger immune health.

(3) Beneficial For Diabetics

Consuming bitter gourd or bitter gourd juice regulates our blood sugar level and helps in managing diabetes.

Because of its low glycemic index,  it slows down the sugar release into the bloodstream, prevents a sudden spike in blood sugar level, and thus regulates blood sugar level.

Dietary fibers in the bitter gourd reduce the rate at which sugar gets absorbed by the bloodstream and helps in managing diabetes.

Polypeptide-P, a phytonutrient in the bitter gourd mimics the action of insulin and lowers the blood sugar levels in diabetic patients.

Other phytochemicals like triterpene, protein, steroid, alkaloid, lipid, and certain phenolic compounds also plays an important role in regulating blood sugar level. 

Even with the anti-diabetic properties of the bitter gourd, consume them in moderation as excessive intake may drop blood sugar to an abnormally low level, thereby increasing the risk of hypoglycemia with symptoms like shakiness, dizziness, sweating, hunger, fast heartbeat, and irritability.
